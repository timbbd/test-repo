Simple Customer Lifetime Value Calculator

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) is an estimate of the total profit a customer will generate over their entire relationship with your company. It gauges the long term health of your companies relationships with your customers, and is useful for predicting how much you should spend in marketing to acquire new customers.

There are many different ways to calculate CLV, and those calculations can range from simplistic to incredibly advanced. With that said, my CLV calculator is very simplistic, and is best suited for the “mom and pop shop” small businesses.

The final CLV calculation is an estimate on how much you should spend to acquire each new customer.

For this calculation, you will need -

  • - Your Total Revenue from Last Year
  • - Amount of Customers That Made a Purchase Last Year
  • - Total Number of Orders you had Last Year
